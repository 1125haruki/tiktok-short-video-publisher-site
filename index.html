Short Video Publisher

Original short videos,
prepared once and published cleanly.

Short Video Publisher is a lightweight publishing support app for creators. It prepares original short-form video assets, captions, thumbnails, and scheduling metadata, then connects authorized social accounts to streamline delivery.

What It Does

Helps creators send original videos to connected platforms with clear account selection, editable metadata, and posting support.

Current TikTok Scope

Initial TikTok integration is designed around the Content Posting Upload API. Users review and complete the final publish flow where required.
