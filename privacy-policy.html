Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-12

This policy describes how Short Video Publisher collects, uses, and protects information when creators connect their social accounts and upload original short-form videos for publishing support.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers required to connect supported platforms
  • Access tokens and refresh tokens needed to publish on behalf of an authorized user
  • Video file URLs, captions, hashtags, thumbnails, and scheduling metadata
  • Operational logs such as publish status, timestamps, and API response metadata

2. How We Use Information

  • To upload and publish original content to connected creator accounts
  • To schedule posts, track success or failure, and support troubleshooting
  • To maintain service stability, prevent abuse, and improve reliability

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers and platform APIs required to deliver the publishing workflow, or when required by law.
